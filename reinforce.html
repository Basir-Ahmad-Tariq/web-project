Reinforcement Learning


Reinforcement learning is a type of Machine learning where an agent learns to make decisions by interacting with an environment. It receives feedback in the form of rewards or penalties for its actions and aims to maximize the cumulative reward over time. This approach is commonly used in robotics, game playing, and autonomous systems.
