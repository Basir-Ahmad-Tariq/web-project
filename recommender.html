Recommender Systems


Recommender systems are Machine learning machine learning models designed to suggest relevant items to users based on their preferences, behavior, or past interactions. They can be content-based (relying on item features), collaborative filtering (leveraging user-item interactions), or hybrid (combining both). Common examples include movie recommendations on Netflix or product suggestions on Amazon.
